# Product Vision

## What Court Reportcard Is

A proofreading tool built specifically for court reporters, scopists, and
proofreaders. Upload a transcript, get a list of flagged issues with suggested
fixes, decide what to accept or ignore, export a clean copy.

It is a second set of eyes — not a replacement for the human. The user owns
every change.

## What It Is Not

- Not a CAT software replacement
- Not a formatting tool
- Not an AI writing assistant
- Not a document management platform (yet)

## Who It Is For

**Primary persona: The Court Reporter**
Zoe is the model user. She uses Case CAT, exports transcripts as .txt or .rtf,
and wants a fast sanity check before sending a rough draft or final copy. She
does not want to be told obvious things she already knows — she wants the
subtle errors caught (wrong homophone, missing word, steno brief that mapped
wrong). She is not technical. She trusts tools that feel personal and
professional, not corporate.

**Secondary persona: The Scopist**
A contractor who edits rough drafts for multiple reporters. Time is money.
Wants to catch errors faster, not be slowed down by noise. High tolerance for
precision (false positives are annoying), lower tolerance for missed errors
(those cost reputation).

**Secondary persona: The Proofreader**
Final pass before a transcript is certified. Needs confidence that nothing
slipped through. Values completeness over speed.

## What Success Looks Like

**Beta success (now):** 30+ real users who have uploaded at least one transcript
and given feedback. At least 5 who say unprompted they would pay for it.

**Launch success:** Parizo Labs LLC set up, payment flow live, 50+ paying
users, product catching errors that matter without generating noise.

**Long-term:** The go-to proofreading tool for the court reporting community.
Known by name in the Facebook groups and professional forums. Trusted because
Brandon replies to every email and ships fixes based on real feedback.

## The Privacy Promise (non-negotiable)

- Transcripts are never used to train anything
- Auto-deleted after 90 days
- All data is secure and encrypted
- Users can delete their transcripts at any time

This must never be violated and must always be communicated clearly.
